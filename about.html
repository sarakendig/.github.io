Hey there!

My name is Sara Kendig and I am a Pennsylvania based full-stack developer. I first developed a love for coding as a teenager as I spent hours designing elaborate themes for my social media profiles. At 17 I started to pursue an interest in photo editing and graphic arts. By 19 I had create my first website for Photoshop resources. Unfortunately, by age 22 my battle with invisible illness would prove to be too much and cause me to put my passion on hold. Thanks to a few long years of hard work and dedication, I would be able to overcome what has been holding me back and happily announce that I am officially in remission. This wonderful news allowed me to go on to have two beautiful children and pursue my dreams. On December 18, 2020, I graduated from General Assembly's software enginnering immersive with a certificate in software engineering. I am excited to see what the future brings.
